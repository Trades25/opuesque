A surreal exploration of duality, Dual Realms captures the interplay between the mechanical and the organic, the familiar and the enigmatic. The piece portrays a face fractured into two perspectives, merging soft human expressions with cold, metallic elements. Each layer peels back the boundaries of identity and reality, inviting viewers to ponder the fluidity of self and the many personas we wear. This digital artwork is a haunting reminder that there is more to see beneath the surface, a gateway to both inner consciousness and the age of AI.
